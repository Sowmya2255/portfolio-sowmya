About Me

Hello! I'm Sowmya S, an Automotive Embedded Engineer passionate about developing safe and sustainable embedded software solutions that bridge hardware and software in modern vehicles. I hold a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) and specialize in building secure software for braking and steering systems. My technical expertise includes programming in C , working with AUTOSAR architecture, and adhering to ASPICE and ISO 26262 safety standards.

I have hands-on experience with the TASKING Compiler, Inspector Tool, and other industry-standard IDEs. I’ve contributed to projects aligned with ISO 26262 Part 8, specifically clauses 11 and 12. Additionally, I’ve performed stack analysis for critical automotive applications using the AbsInt Tool, ensuring compliance and robustness in safety-critical systems.

Currently, I work as an Associate Embedded Engineer at ZF Technologies in collaboration with Neotech Global. My focus lies in developing reliable software for IBC projects involving both AI and embedded systems. I’m actively involved in test automation, utilizing platforms like Azure DevOps and Jenkins, and performing embedded development on Tricore Microcontrollers. I enjoy solving rare edge-case issues, conducting system-level test analysis, and contributing to innovations in vehicle safety.
